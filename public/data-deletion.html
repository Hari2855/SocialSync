Data Deletion Instructions

If you would like to delete your account and associated data from our app, SocialSync, please follow the steps below:

Steps to Delete Your Data:

  1. Log in to Your Account: Open the SocialSync app on your device and use your credentials to log in.
  2. Navigate to Account Settings: Go to the Settings section, and select Privacy or Account Settings.
  3. Delete Your Account: Find the option labeled Delete Account or Data Deletion, and confirm your action.

Alternative Method:

Note: Once your data is deleted, it cannot be recovered. Some data may be retained for legal or compliance purposes as required by applicable laws.
